FAQ

About Timmy

Timmy is discord but filled with minigames. The purposes of Timmy is to add a little fun to our user's discord server

How can i invite Timmy ?

You can invite Timmy to your server by pressing the invite button at the navbar.

How do i call Timmy ?

You can call timmy with the prefix "T-" followed by the command. You can use "T-whatcanyoudo" to see what can Timmy do, or use "T-howto [command]" to get details of a specific command
